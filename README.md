# Temptare
**"To Try"** or **"To Attempt"** in Latin - This is my attempt at game development.

Temptare is a virtual reality video game intended to be played on the Meta Quest 2.
It is a first-person shooter with a maze and a shooting range.
The purpose of the game is to educate players on firearm safety in a fun and, more importantly, risk free environment.
Teaching firearm safety using the Meta Quest 2 allows players to experience hands-on learning without the possibility of injury or death.
Players are rewarded for shooting hostile, red targets and penalized for shooting friendly, green targets.
The player will also be penalized for aiming at themselves. The number of times friendly targets were aimed at is recorded
and shown to the player at the end of the game.
